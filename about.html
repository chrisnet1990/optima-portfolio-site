Understanding Optima Portfolio

Optima Portfolio is an educational engine built to bridge the gap between retail investing and institutional-grade mathematics. Our mission is to provide high-quality financial analysis tools for the next generation of investors.

How the Optimization Works

Most investors pick stocks based on recent hype. Our app uses Modern Portfolio Theory (MPT). By analyzing 10 years of history (over 1.4 million data points), we calculate the Efficient Frontier.

The Monte Carlo Method: We don't just calculate one outcome. We simulate 2,000 different ways to split your money across your chosen stocks to find the one with the highest Sharpe Ratio.

User Guide

  1. Select Market: Choose between Indian (Nifty 50) or US (S&P 500) data sets.
  2. Pick Stocks: Select at least 5 companies to allow for proper diversification and mathematical variance.
  3. Analyze: Review the Sharpe Ratio and Alpha to see if your strategy beats the benchmark index over time.
