Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

At Optima Portfolio, accessible from www.optimaportfolio.com, one of our main priorities is the privacy of our visitors. This document outlines the types of information collected and how it is utilized within our educational framework.

1. Consent

By using our website, you hereby consent to our Privacy Policy and agree to its terms.

2. Information We Collect

Optima Portfolio is an educational backtesting tool.

  • Personal Data: We do not require account registration and do not collect personal identification information such as names or email addresses.
  • Portfolio Inputs: Data processed within the app (like stock picks) is handled client-side and is not permanently stored on our servers.

3. Log Files

We follow standard log file procedures. Information collected includes IP addresses, browser types, and date/time stamps. This data is not linked to personally identifiable information.

4. Google DoubleClick DART Cookie

As a third-party vendor, Google uses DART cookies to serve ads based on your visit to www.optimaportfolio.com. You may decline these via the Google ad network Privacy Policy.

5. Advertising Partners

Our advertising partners, such as Google AdSense, may use cookies and web beacons. Their privacy policies can be reviewed on their respective platforms.

6. GDPR & CCPA Rights

We respect all data protection rights. Users are entitled to access, rectification, and erasure. Since we do not collect personal data by design, your privacy is maintained by default.

7. Contact

For questions regarding this policy, please reach out via the administration links on our main platform.
